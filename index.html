Us

The Meet
We met the very first hour of the very first day of our pre-orientation program at Tufts University. We were friends throughout all of college, biking through Boston and Cambridge on the hunt for good seafood and constantly making Hodgdon Hall runs for the fresh made bread. We went on vacations together, made dinner together, and we definitely weren't dating.
The Date
After graduation, we planned a road trip across the country together. After 3 weeks of 24/7 hiking, driving, camping, and site seeing, we still didn't want to spend any time apart. Finally, we admitted our feelings to one another and went on our first date. It was full of laughter, hand holding, and some very adventurous Ethiopian food.
The Proposal
Four years after that first date, we were celebrating Adam's 27th birthday at one of our favorite Boston restaurants. After dessert, Adam got down on one knee in front of the entire restaurant and proposed. Even though I forgot to say yes, he took the vigorous nodding as a good sign that I was on board.
